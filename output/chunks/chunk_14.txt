. 30. Pre-Hospitalization Medical Expenses means Medical Expenses incurred immediately before the Insured Person is Hospitalized, provided that: i. Such Medical Expenses are incurred for the same condition for which the Insured Person’s Hospitalization was required, and ii. The In-patient Hospitalization claim for such Hospitalization is admissible by the Insurance Company. 31. Post Hospitalization Medical Expenses means Medical Expenses incurred immediately after the Insured Person is discharged from the hospital, provided that: i. Such Medical Expenses are incurred for the same condition for which the Insured Person’s Hospitalization was required, and ii. The In-patient Hospitalization claim for such Hospitalization is admissible by the Insurance Company 32.
